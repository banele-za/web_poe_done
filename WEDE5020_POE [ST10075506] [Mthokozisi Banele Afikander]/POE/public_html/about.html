About Us

Future Stars Development Club was created in 2010 in the township of Tsakane by two friends who sought out to keep children off the streets after the rampart increase of drug usage by the youth.

The initial goal was to develop young children into professional footballers after the hosting of the FIFA World Cup in 2010 right her in South Africa inspired a lot of children and showed them that they can too, play on the big stage of the beautiful game.

...But the organisation has grown to be far more than that, we now lecture, coach and teach arts and culture, including dancing, music and painting, helping the youth realize their creativity in a useful manner.

Our Mission

Future Stars is a local powerful lighting nonprofit bringing the youth the power to raise themselves out of the darkness of poverty and drug abuse as well as strive to have a better future not defined by the place they come from.


Our Vision

Eradication of drug abuse through extramural activities.
