Our Work

As our name suggests, we work by improving the community in a number of ways, a small team of volunteers help by coaching, tutoring and as well as mentoring children from a young age.

We currently have 3 football coaches who teach children how to improve and become better footballers, these 3 coaches coach 4 different age groups which consist of almost ONE HUNDRED children with the under 10s division having almost 40 children

Our team of tutors only consits of 2 people, they are involved with helping the children with their homework and schoolwork with the help of a local school offering space in unused classrooms after hours. The children alternate between football, dance and tuition from monday to thursday with friday being a day off and saturday being the day for matches and other activities
